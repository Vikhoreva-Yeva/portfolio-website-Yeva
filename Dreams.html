DREAMS

This book "Dreams" was created with the aim of trying to give dreams a physical form, to capture the feeling of sleep, dreams and this mystical world.

The technique chosen was woodcut and working with a single board, with the help of which 4 layers were made, so each layer was cut on top of the previous one. When each of them is layered, it conveys the feeling of being in a dream, or the change of events in each of them. So layer by layer, the dreams appeared ...
