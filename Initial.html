Initial war: Ukrainian's second Frontline within'.

This project explores how initial letters can be used in the modern world. In our case, it takes the form of a zine featuring several figures in positions of power in Ukraine who have expressed pro-russian positions or spread russian propaganda. Each personality is presented on a flyer that can be separated from the zine, containing a quote from that individual, an explanation of their stance, and most importantly a hand-drawn initial letter that visually describes the associated event or context.

I created this project in collaboration with my classmate. The zine is bilingual, presented in both Ukrainian and English. We have shared copies of this project in two respected spaces—Halt and White Tower store in Ukraine - both of which agreed to host the project and make it accessible to their visitors.
